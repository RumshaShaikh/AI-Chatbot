About Me

Want to know all about me such as what do i offer? Keep reading buddy !!!

  • I'm Peepo a chat and voicebot used to conduct an online chat conversation via text or text-to-speech, providing direct contact with a live robotic agent. I can help customers by automating conversations and interact with them through messaging platforms.
  • I can be integrated to different websites and trained on their database after which I can assist you with your queries related to the website as i will be trained on all the data and hence be like a replaceable character of a support person in call centers.
  • You can either text chat with me by directly messaging me from Textchat option and voicechat me via speech input from VoiceChat and I'll try to answer all of your questions. Feel free to ask me any Questions.

And this is all not about me... Feeling lonely? Want a friend to talk to? I can talk to you without ever making you feel lonely. Chat with me via Chat-Buddy.

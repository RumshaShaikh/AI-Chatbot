What is a Chatbot?

At the most basic level, a chatbot is a computer program that simulates and processes human conversation, allowing humans to interact with digital devices as if they were communicating with a real person.


What is NLP?

Natural language processing (NLP) refers to the branch of computer science—and artificial intelligence — concerned with giving computers the ability to understand text and spoken words in much the same way human beings can.


What is NLP Chatbot?

An NLP based chatbot is a computer program or artificial intelligence that communicates with a customer via textual or sound methods. Such programs are often designed to support clients on websites or via phone.
